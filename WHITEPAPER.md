## Abstract
StreetCoin (STC) is a community-driven ERC-20 token created for educational purposes, open-source learning, and transparent experimentation on the Ethereum blockchain.

This project is not an investment vehicle and does not promise profit or financial returns.

## Motivation
The goal of StreetCoin is to provide a simple and real-world example of how ERC-20 tokens work in practice, including deployment, verification, documentation, and community interaction.

## Project Scope
- Educational experimentation
- Open-source development
- Transparent on-chain activity
- Community-based discussion and learning

## Token Overview
- Name: StreetCoin
- Symbol: STC
- Network: Ethereum
- Standard: ERC-20

## Distribution
StreetCoin does not conduct token sales, ICOs, or fundraising events.  
There is no private allocation, seed sale, or investor distribution.

## Transparency
All development activity, documentation, and updates are publicly available via GitHub.  
The smart contract is deployed and verifiable on Ethereum.

## Disclaimer
StreetCoin (STC) is **not** a financial product.  
It does **not** represent an investment, security, or promise of value.

Participation is voluntary and for educational purposes only.
